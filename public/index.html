CivicLens Patterns, not verdicts.

The congressional paper trail, reconstructed.

CivicLens reconstructs what members of Congress did — trades, votes, sponsorships, and donors — from primary sources, and flags where the timing or the money lines up in ways that stand out from chance. Every flag is reproducible and links back to the filing it came from. It reports patterns, not verdicts.
